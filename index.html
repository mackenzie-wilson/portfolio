About Me

I am currently employed by the federal government, specializing in business intelligence and project management. While my work experience has been nothing short of amazing, I'd like to transition to a faster-paced work environment in a customer-engaging role. The most rewarding project I have lead so far focused on working closely with users to design and develop a business intelligence tool for process improvement analytics. The process of iteratively solving user problems and contributing to creating something out of nothing is what I am looking for.

I strive to be a lifelong learner and seriously value the self-learning I do in my free time through projects, online courses, and reading at an average pace of one book per week. Navigate to my projects page to view some of the work I have done in the past or things I am currently working on. You can also find a list of some of my favourite nonfiction books that span a myriad of disciplines in my bookshelf.
